Frequently Asked Question

Q. How can I attend an event?

To attend an event you need to log-in or sign up to Codzilla, then simply click the button attend on the event page. The event should now show in ‘My Events section’ under your profile.

Q. How can I remove my attendance to an event?

You are unable to attend or you have decided not to go to an event. Go to ‘My events’ and click on the menu to remove yourself from the event or simply click the button ‘attending’ to change your status.

Q. How can I follow a group?

You can click on the star icon to follow a group. You can see your groups in ‘My Groups’ section under your profile. To view all the events associated with the group, just go onto the group page.

Q. How can I unfollow a group?

Go to ‘My Groups’ section and click on the menu to unfollow the group or simply click the button “Followed’ on the group page.

Q. How can I create an event?

You can either click the calendar icon in the top menu or go to My Events to add an event.

Q. Can I create online events?

Yes you can create online events by selecting the event type when adding an event.

Q. Where can I see my events?

Go to ‘My events’ section under your profile to see your events as an organiser or a participant.

Q. How can I edit / cancel an event?

Go to ‘My events’ and click on the menu to select the relevant options.

Q. How do I delete an event?

First you will need to cancel your events so that your attendees are notified, then you can delete the event by going to ‘My Event section’ and click on the menu to select the delete option.

Q. How can I inform my attendees my event has been postponed?

When you cancel your event, your attendees will be immediately notified and they will be able to see the cancellation message in their notifications.

Q. Do I have to create a group to add an event?

No you don’t have to create a group to add events to Codzilla. However, adding a group makes it easier to gather followers and add co-administrators to your events.

Q. Where do I see my groups?

Go to ‘My Group’ section under your profile page to see your groups as an organiser or as a member.

Q. How can I remove my notifications?

Go to your profile section and update your preferences accordingly.

Q. Can I delete my profile?

Yes you can delete your profile by going to your profile page. The delete button will be at the bottom of the page.

Q. Can I add co-adminastors to my events?

You can only add co-administrators to your events via your group. To add a co-administrator go to my group then enter the email address of your co-admin. If your event is not associated with a group, go to your event page and add the relevant group.
